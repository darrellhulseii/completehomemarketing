Questions

Good to know

Are you Frontier or EarthLink? +
Neither. Complete Home Today is an independent Authorized Sales Agent for Frontier and an Authorized Reseller for EarthLink. We help you compare and sign up for service, while the provider you choose handles the network, billing, installation, and support.
Which providers and speeds can I get? +
We offer Frontier Fiber (200 Mbps up to 7 Gig) and EarthLink (100 Mbps up to 5 Gig fiber, plus a wireless 5G Home Internet option up to 300 Mbps). The exact providers and speeds available depend on your address — that's what the availability check confirms.
What are the current offers? +
Frontier is running 2 Months "On Us" (1 Gig or 500M, through June 30, 2026), a $400 Samsung credit on 1 Gig+, 3 months of FOX One for eligible Verizon mobile customers, and a $15/mo Mobile + Home discount. EarthLink offers introductory pricing on its 1 Gig and 500M fiber plans and a $59.95/mo 5G Home Internet plan. Offers change often — your agent confirms current details.
What fees should I expect? +
Frontier prices shown reflect a $10 AutoPay discount and don't include taxes and surcharges. EarthLink plans include a $14.95/mo router rental, a one-time $24.95 install fee, and a $79.95 processing and handling fee due at signup. Your agent reviews every fee before you commit.
Is there a contract? +
It depends on the plan. Some Frontier promotions include a 12-month term, and an early termination fee may apply if service is cancelled within that period. EarthLink's 5G Home Internet requires no contract. Your agent will explain the terms for whichever plan you choose.
Do I need Verizon mobile service? +
No. Frontier and EarthLink internet work fully on their own. If you do have an eligible Verizon postpaid mobile plan, you may unlock extra Frontier savings — the $15/mo Mobile + Home discount, Verizon Perks, and the FOX One offer. It's a bonus, not a requirement.
Do you offer home security? +
Yes. We offer ADT-monitored home security through Direct Protection Security, an ADT Authorized Dealer, with three packages — Bronze, Silver, and Gold. Equipment and 24/7 monitoring are quoted for your home; just pick a package on the availability form and your agent handles the rest.
Is the pricing on this site final? +
Offers and pricing change periodically and vary by address. Your local Complete Home Today agent confirms exact, current pricing and terms for your home at the point of sale.

Rep FAQ

Questions about joining

Is this a W-2 job or 1099? +
This is a 1099 independent contractor role. You are not an employee of Complete Home Today LLC. There is no base salary, no guaranteed hours, and no employee benefits. You are responsible for your own taxes, transportation, and expenses. The engagement is structured in compliance with California AB5 (Labor Code §2775 et seq.) and the Borello multi-factor test — you control your own schedule, routes, and sales approach.
What products will I sell? +
Frontier Fiber Internet (200M through 7 Gig, plus EarthLink plans) and ADT-monitored home security through Direct Protection Security. Selling both on the same visit maximizes your per-door earnings. Current Frontier promos include 2 months free internet, a $400 Samsung Best Buy credit, and FOX One — strong tools that make the pitch easier.
How and when do I get paid? +
Commission is earned per confirmed installation — not per pitch, not per signature. Payment timelines and the full rate structure are outlined in your IC agreement before you sign. Nothing is verbal.
What if an install gets cancelled after I close the deal? +
Chargeback rules are defined in your IC agreement. You will know exactly under what conditions a commission is clawed back before you ever close your first deal. No surprises after the fact.
Do I need prior sales experience? +
No. We provide in-depth pitch training for those who need support and travel options. Prior experience helps, but what matters most is a self-starter mindset and willingness to put in the reps.
What territories are available? +
Anywhere Frontier or EarthLink is available. Because EarthLink offers internet in all 50 states, there's opportunity throughout the United States. Fiber-optic internet is more limited — it isn't offered in every territory — but the majority of California is covered by one provider or the other, and fiber reaches roughly 28 states through Frontier and 21 through EarthLink. Your exact territory is set during your phone screen, based on your location and what's serviceable nearby.
Is this compliant with California AB5? +
Yes. The IC agreement is drafted in compliance with California Labor Code §2775 et seq. (AB5) and the Borello multi-factor test. As an independent contractor, you are free to set your own schedule, choose your own routes, and control your own sales approach. You are not subject to behavioral control by the Company.
